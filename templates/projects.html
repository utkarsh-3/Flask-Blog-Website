Pothole Detection System
Aim : To lower the road accidents caused due to the potholes on the roads.
Duration : Feb 2020 - April 2020
Working :
1. Citizens will upload the pictures of potholes.
2. Web Application will fetch image along with its GPS location.
3. The custom Machine Learning model will validate the image.
4. The verified images are sent to Municipality.
5. Further the citizens can keep track of progress of their uploads.
Tools : Flask , SQLite3 , Tensorflow , Google Teachable Machine , HTML GPS API


Urban Street Cleanliness
Aim : To improve cleanliness in city with the help of technology
Duration : Jan 2020 - April 2020
Working :
1. Citizens will raise a ticket uploading a picture of garbage with location data.
2. Web ans Android Application will fetch image along with its metadata.
3. The AWS Recognition service will validate the image and detect garbage.
4. The verified images are sorted according to areas and sent to Municipality.
5. Further the citizens can keep track of progress of their tickets and submit feedback.
Tools : Ionic , Angular , AWS Recognition , Firebase

Event Management Application
Aim : To improve registration process for events
Duration : Aug 2019 - Nov 2019
Working :
1. The participents are asked to send their photo to store in database.
2. On event day, participents are validated according to solo or group registration with android appication.
3. Various functionalities are provided according to different authorizations.
4. The application will overall help manage the event easily.
